Mastery

The best way to learn, is to teach - let your students teach you and their peers what they know.

Using Mastery, students can consolidate their knowledge into a one minute narrated video which teachers can instantly provide with rich feedback. Videos can be shared as a resource to classmates or kept private as a revision resource.

Frequently Asked Question's

  • How much does it cost?

    It’s completely free to consolidate learning with Mastery.

    Students can consolidate their knowledge into a one minute narrated video which teachers can instantly provide rich feedback. All for free.

    In the future we’ll have some paid upgrades designed for whole school use, but the basics of Mastery videos will continue to be free.

  • Is Mastery just a video sharing tool?

    Mastery is a video-based learning platform, letting both teachers and students to create, collaborate and share knowledge. It gives students an opportunity to consolidate their knowledge into a 60 second narrated video. Mastery creates experiences like this.

  • How can I let my student use Mastery without being exposed to other users?

    Videos can be private to the student, or shared with peers in a class or school. All videos are moderated by teachers before being allowed to be shared. Videos that show the learners identity are discouraged and the teacher always has the final control about how far the video can be shared.

  • What do I need to make it work?

    Students need to have access to a smartphone that can record video.

  • How do I add students to my class?

    Once students have downloaded ‘share a class’ code with fellow students and teachers individually approve requests to join the class.
